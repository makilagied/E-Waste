About Us

Our Vision is to re-use and recycle the electronic wastes that are produced every day in our homes or offices.

  • We encouraging people to collect their non usable electrical and electronic devices so as to conserve the environment and re-use them in a better way.

We are a group of Computer Scientists who are passionate in conserving environment in this digital world by reducing amount of e-waste that are produced everyday due to advancement of science and technologies. By collecting electrical and electronic waste that are produced in home place and offices.

Why E-Waste

E-wastes are electrical and electronic waste. These includes computers, TV appliances, laptops, mobile phones, smartphones, washing machine, fridges electrical toys, Kettler and etc.

  • 01 Creating a safe environment for the comming generation

    E waste are found both in offices and household. Research show that E waste is growing exponentially because global consumer demand continues to increase. Moreover, Technology uptake and shorter replacement cycles are contributing to the growth of E waste.

  • 02 Recycling of the devices

    The global E waste monitor in 2017 show that E waste has grown to 44.7 million metric tons annually. But only 20% of e waste generated were documented to be collected and recycled leaving behind 76% you can do the math. The missing percent is likely to be dumped or recycled under inferior condition.

  • 03 Reusing devices and circuit boards for alternative use

    As we see that E waste is a global challenge but you can be the part of solution by collect you E waste product today. Follow the link and fill the form in order for us to come and collect E waste for proper disposal. Me and you can make this world the better place for many generations to come.
